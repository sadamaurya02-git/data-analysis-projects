**Key Insights:**

- Operational Efficiency: Hire seasonal staff and establish regional inventories to manage order surges.

- Delivery Optimization: Investigate delay causes and expand local warehouses to reduce logistics costs.

- Payment Strategy: Encourage credit and EMI payments through cashback and transparent reward plans.

- Regional Focus: Prioritize SP state for investment and growth opportunities.
