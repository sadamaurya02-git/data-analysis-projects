**Netflix: Data Exploration & Visualization**

**About:**
Netflix is a global streaming leader with over 220M subscribers and a vast library of movies and TV shows across genres and regions.

**Objective:**
Analyze the content catalog to identify trends and insights that guide production, localization, and content strategy.

- Strengthen U.S. content and expand regional productions (e.g., India).

- Invest in local-language and mature content to attract new audiences.

- Balance family-friendly (PG/PG-13) and nostalgic content to engage all age groups.

- Focus on unique releases and interactive storytelling to stand out from competitors.
