**Walmart: Confidence Interval & Central Limit Theorem (CLT)**

**About:**
Walmart is a global retail leader with over 100 million customers worldwide. This project analyzes customer purchase behavior to understand spending differences by gender, age, and other demographic factors.

**Objective:**
Apply statistical inference using confidence intervals and the Central Limit Theorem to uncover spending trends and guide marketing strategies.

**Key Insights:**

- High-Spenders: Offer premium deals and loyalty rewards to boost retention.

- Gender Trends: Male customers show higher spending during Black Friday — target with special promotions.

- Age Group 26–35: Frequent shoppers — ideal for volume-based offers.

Occupation Segmentation:

- Occupations 0, 4, 7 → volume-based offers & loyalty programs.

- Occupations 12, 17 → premium product upsells.

- Operational Focus: Stock high-demand items in key regions and offer cashback bundles for City Category A.
